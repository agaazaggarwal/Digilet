How we are using Cloud Computing to our advantage

"Wearable technology and cloud computing is all about collecting and analysing data." What can cloud computing be used for? Cloud computing allows people to access cloud-based communication tools such as calendars and emails. Also, messaging and calling apps such as WhatsApp and Skype are all built on cloud infrastructure. And you can access you messages and notifications from the screen on DigiLet 1.0.Moreover, Your Personal data like your name, your family member's phone number and your age are uploaded to your bracelet using cloud computing and then in case you are a victim of any accident, the other person can access it on his mobile phone and call you family members. With this feature it wil be really beneficial for the family members to know about the location of the accident.

How we are using NFC technology to our advantage

As we all know that Near Field communication(NFC) is the new future and yes, it is embedded in our DigiLet 1.0. You can put pretty much anything on it. Our basic idea of embedding NFC was to revolutionalise the idea of attendence in schools. Students can just tap their bracelets on NFC readers which will record their response and send it to their teacher for attendence. And obviosly using NFC Tools people can add their any social handle for sharing purposes. And so when they'll tap their bracelet on the NFC enabled phone of the other person and they'll be able view their social handle. Another idea of using NFC on our bracelet is to make an NFC business card and then use the bracelet to share it with other people.
